Introduction#

The Trans Euro Trail (abbreviated TET) is a beautiful GPX route that spans the whole European continent. At the time of writing its length is somewhere around 100.000 kilometers, and it is constantly growing. Each country has its responsive linesman that curates the GPX, but the community behind is much bigger than a single person!
